Rubik Cube

Use the keyboard to rotate the cube pressing the letter of the rotation using the standart notation. You can read about the notation here: https://www.speedsolving.com/wiki/index.php/3x3x3_notation

Clockwise rotation: normal key

Anti clockwise rotation: Upper case key (use the SHIFT key)

The most common rotations have buttons on the right to be compatible with devices without keyboard (smart phones, tablets)
